Registered Cooperatives

Kilimo House Bomet works closely with farmers' cooperatives and SACCOs to promote sustainable development and economic empowerment in the agricultural sector.

1. Bomet Dairy Cooperative

Milk collection and marketing for 2,000+ dairy farmers across Bomet County.

2. Kapset Tea SACCO

Provides credit services, farming support, and tea marketing assistance to smallholder growers.

3. Chepalungu Farmers Union

Supports farmers producing maize, beans, and horticultural crops while promoting agribusiness practices.

4. Women in Agribusiness Group

Empowers women in poultry farming, kitchen gardening, and agri-financing through training and seed funding.

5. Sot Tea Cooperative

Assists tea farmers with extension services, collection logistics, and fair pricing strategies.

6. Longisa Potato Growers

Provides potato farmers with training in seed selection, storage, and market linkage opportunities.

7. Kipsonoi SACCO

Offers savings and credit products to dairy farmers and supports livestock productivity programs.

8. Youth Agritech Network

Trains youth on agribusiness, greenhouse farming, and digital agriculture for job creation.

9. Silibwet Multipurpose Cooperative

Handles cereals, dairy, and agro-input supplies while promoting cooperative values.

10. Tenwek Coffee Growers Union

Provides coffee farmers with planting support, training, and access to export markets.
