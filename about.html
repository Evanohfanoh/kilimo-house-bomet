About Kilimo House Bomet

Kilimo House Bomet is the official platform dedicated to promoting agriculture and cooperatives in Bomet County. We provide services, resources, and support to farmers, SACCOs, and agricultural stakeholders across the county.

Our mission is to improve food security, drive sustainable agribusiness, and enhance cooperative performance through digital access and government programs.
